Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

The website is working for 24/7 but only the donation camps are organized at a particular time that is mentioned on the website.

How to organize a donation camp?

If you are willing to organize a donation camp reach out to us by mail or contact provided in "Contact" section and we will be glad to work with you.

How can i upload from my side?

By clicking on the tab "Feel free to donate , you will be redirected to a page where you can fill your details and information regarding the food and location".

Where to provide and collect food?

User will just have to upload the details and location, and our team will collect food from you and provide it to the needy people.

What is cost of food delivery?

As it an act of kindness and help to needy the food is delivered to them at NO cost.

Who is eligible to join our team?

Anyone willing to help the poor and needy people around can reach out to us and join our team to build a step towards humanity.
